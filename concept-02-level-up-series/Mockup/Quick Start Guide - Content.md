## INSIDE RIGHT — 3 Things to Try Tonight

### Tonight. Not someday. Tonight.

**1. Play 5 cards from Start the Talk** (10 min)
- Shuffle the yellow deck.
- Youth draws first. Read the card aloud.
- Both answer — honestly.
- If you hit a **Swap Card** (black border), answer as each other.
- No right answers. No judging. Just talk.

**2. Pick 1 Challenge Card** (2 min)
- Fan out the coral cards face down.
- Each person picks one.
- Read it. Commit to it. Put it on the scoreboard.
- Duration is on the card — 3 days, 7 days, or 14 days.

**3. Scan the "How Are You?" QR** (1 min)
- Just the youth. Just for you.
- Scan → pick how you're feeling → get one small action.
- Nobody else sees it. No data stored.

**Total: ~15 minutes to get started.**

## BACK — Everything You Need to Know

### Start the Talk — How to Play

**32 cards. 4 types.**

| Type | Cards | How to spot it | What to do |
|------|-------|---------------|-----------|
| **Icebreaker** | 1–10 | Yellow bg, yellow border | Light, funny. Great openers. "Never have I ever..." |
| **Real Talk** | 11–20 | Yellow bg, yellow border | Goes a bit deeper. Still "Never have I ever..." |
| **Deeper** | 21–26 | Coral/pink bg, red border | Open-ended questions. No game format — just talk. |
| **Swap** | 27–32 | Yellow bg, black border | **Joker cards.** Answer as the other person. |

**Rules:**
- Shuffle all cards together.
- Youth draws first.
- Read the card aloud. Both answer.
- Play 5–10 cards per session. Don't rush.
- Hit a Swap Card? Answer as each other. See how well you really know them.
- No wrong answers. No "but actually." Just listen.

### Start Simple — Challenge Cards

**16 challenge cards across 4 categories.**

**Better Sleep**
- Phone down by 9:30pm (7 days)
- Same bedtime for 7 nights (7 days)
- No screens 30 min before bed (3 days)
- Dim the lights after 8pm (7 days)

**Eat Better**
- Eat breakfast every day (7 days)
- Drink 8 glasses of water (7 days)
- Cook one meal together (3 days)
- Try one new fruit or veg (7 days)

**Move Your Body**
- 10-minute walk after dinner (7 days)
- Take the stairs for 2 weeks (14 days)
- Stretch for 5 min before bed (3 days)
- Dance to one song a day (7 days)

**Mind & Focus**
- No social media for 1 hour after waking (7 days)
- 5-minute breathing before bed (7 days)
- Write one thing you're grateful for daily (14 days)
- Have dinner without phones on the table (3 days)

**How to play:**
1. Fan out the coral cards face down.
2. Each person picks one.
3. Read it aloud. Agree to try it.
4. Put both cards on the magnetic scoreboard.
5. Track daily — move the magnetic token across when done.
6. Both play. Nobody gets to nag if both are doing it.

**Scoreboard:**
- Magnetic. Sticks to the fridge.
- Two rows — one for each player.
- Move your token one space per day completed.
- Finish the challenge? Pick a new card.

### Wellness Boards — Daily Habit Trackers

**4 boards. Same system. Different focus.**

| Board | Colour | Example habits |
|-------|--------|---------------|
| Better Sleep | Blue | Lights out by 10:30pm, No caffeine after 3pm |
| Eat Better | Green | One fruit with breakfast, Water before meals |
| Move Your Body | Coral | Walk 10 min, Stretch before bed |
| Mind & Focus | Violet | 5 min quiet time, Gratitude journal |

**How to use:**
1. Pick 1–2 boards to start. Don't do all 4.
2. Choose 2–4 habits per board (pre-printed options or write your own).
3. Each morning or evening, place your magnetic check token on completed habits.
4. Reset weekly. Swap boards as you build confidence.
5. Put them somewhere visible — fridge, desk, door.

---

### "How Are You?" — QR Mood Check-in

**For youth only. Private. No login.**

1. Scan the QR code on the green card.
2. Pick how you're feeling right now (emoji scale).
3. Get one small, practical action suggestion.
4. That's it. No account. No data saved. No one else sees.
5. Scan anytime you want — there's no schedule.

**If you're going through a tough time:**
The check-in will always show you where to get support — including SOS (1800 221 4444), IMH helpline (6389 2222), and ec2.sg for online counselling.

---

### Parent Zone — QR Card

**For parents/caregivers. Separate experience.**

1. Scan the QR code on the purple card.
2. Access practical, peer-to-peer tips from other parents.
3. Short reads: How to listen without fixing. How to ask without interrogating. How to notice without hovering.
4. Not a lecture. Not clinical. Just real.

---

## Care & Storage

- Store cards in the box tray when not in use.
- Magnetic boards and tokens can be wiped clean with a dry cloth.
- Keep QR cards — they don't expire.
- The box is designed to sit on a shelf or countertop. Keep it visible so it gets used.
