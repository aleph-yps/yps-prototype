Play with your parent, relatives, or a buddy.

Hit a Swap Card? Switch roles and answer as each other.

Start Simple — Challenge Card

Play with your parent, a relative, or a buddy.
Pick one challenge card and commit to it for a week.
Choose a magnet to represent yourself, write the challenge on the leaderboard, and place it somewhere visible at home.
Track your progress daily — and see who wins at the end of the week.
